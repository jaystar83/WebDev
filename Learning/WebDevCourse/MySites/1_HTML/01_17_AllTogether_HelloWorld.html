A few facts about the world we live in


The western hemisphere of the Blue Marble, created in 2002. NASA's Earth Observatory

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and is the largest of the terrestrial planets. Unlike the other planets in the solar system that are named after classic deities the Earth's name comes from the Anglo Saxon word "erda" which means ground or soil. The Earth was formed approximated 4.54 billion years ago and us the only known planet to support life.
